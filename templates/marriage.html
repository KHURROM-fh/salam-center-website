Marriage, or Nikah, is a sacred and cherished bond in Islam, symbolizing love, commitment, and mutual respect. At SALAM Islamic Center, we are dedicated to helping our community members embark on this significant journey with ease and blessed beginnings.

Our Imams are here to facilitate your Nikah, an Islamic Marriage, ceremony at the Masjid according to the Islamic practice of marriage principles.

Our team assists with the necessary documentation required for your Nikah, including the Islamic marriage certificate and the legal requirements for marriage in California.
